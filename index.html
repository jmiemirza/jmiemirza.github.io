I am currently a Computer Vision PhD. student advised by Professor Horst Bischof, at TU Graz, Austria. I did my Masters from KIT, Germany in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology (ETIT) and received my Bachelors in Electrical Engineering (EE) from NUST, Pakistan.

My area of research revolves around self-supervised and unsupervised representation learning for domain adaptation. More specifically, I work on making pre-trained neural networks robust to out-of-distribution data on-the-fly at test-time. An interesting use case for my research is making present day autonomous driving architectures (e.g., object detectors) robust to degrading weather conditions as these conditions are encountered during driving.
